Card Effect Breakdown

Activation Requirements & Resolution Steps

1

Cost Payment

Banish from your Extra Deck face-up:

  • 2 XYZ Monsters with the same Rank
  • 1 Fusion Monster

Their combined Levels/Ranks must equal the total number of cards in both players' hands and on the field.

2

Effect Resolution

Return to the Extra Deck:

  • 1 XYZ Monster from your banished cards
  • 1 Fusion Monster from your banished cards

Their combined Levels/Ranks must match the Level/Rank of 1 face-up monster your opponent controls.

Then, banish all cards your opponent controls.

Advanced Play Tips

  • Chain Flexibility
    Activate during either player's turn. Not HOPT - can be used multiple times per turn if possible
  • Resource Management
    Returned monsters don't need to be the ones banished for activation
  • Combo Potential
    Pair with Dogmatika cards or Maxx "C" to manipulate card counts
  • Mind Games
    Opponents can't anticipate which monster you'll target for the second effect
