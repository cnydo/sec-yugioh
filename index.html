Simultaneous Equation Cannon Effects

  1. Banish 1 pair of same-rank XYZ monsters and 1 Fusion monster (3 Extra Deck cards) whose combined Levels/Ranks equal the total number of cards in both players' hands and fields.
  2. Return 1 banished XYZ and 1 banished Fusion monster to the Extra Deck whose combined Levels/Ranks equal a face-up monster your opponent controls, then banish all cards they control.

Key Tips

  • The returned banished monsters don't need to be those banished by this effect
  • This card's effect isn't Hard Once Per Turn (HOPT)
  • Both effects resolve simultaneously - opponents can't anticipate your secondary targets
  • Chain manipulation (Dogmatika Punishment, Maxx "C", etc.) can control total card counts
